controlled by this law under the free competition.

price increases , the person ability decreases. With this decrease, the quantity of the 
purchased units will be decreased.  
 
 Supply                                  العرض  
It is the quantity  of the produced units that the producer can offer in the market. 
When the product price increases , the producer increases the produc tion to g et the 
maximum profit.

 What is Cost Accounting ? 
It is a process of collecting, analysing , summari sing and evaluating various alternative 
courses of action. Its goal is to advise the management on the most appropriate 
course of action based on the  cost efficiency  and capability . 
 
 
 Cost Elements  
There are two types of costs:  
 
A- Direct Cost (Variable Cost)      الكلف المتغيرة  
It includes the costs that are used in the production process. This includes the 
following costs:  
- Raw materials costs                 كلف المواد األولية  
- Costs of man -power                كلف اإليدي العاملة  
- Costs of the operation and maintenance of the machines and tools used in 
production (with the cost of the depreciation)    مصاريف تشغيل و صيانة المعدات و كلف إندثارها  
- Costs of storage, protection and marketing                     كلف الخزن و التسويق  
- Energy costs                        صرفيات إستهالك الطاقة  
 
A- Overhead  Cost ( Fixed  Cost)         الكلف الثابتة

are necessary of the. For example:  
- Costs and expenses of the management office                                      المصاريف اإلدارية  
-  Costs and expenses of the buildings and its depreciation  مصاريف المنشاءات وتكاليفإندثارها
